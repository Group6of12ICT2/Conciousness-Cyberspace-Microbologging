Cyber Grooming
Cyber grooming is the act of developing a trustworthy relationship with a minor online in order to commit a sexual misconduct. "Common interests" can help malefactors get the attention of teens: discussing fashion, art, sports, music, and entertainment or use bullying and trolling (provocations, harassment, psychological manipulation) to act aggressively.

How do you avoid cyber grooming?

  • Don't talk to Strangers Online. Because they might have a bad intentions on you.
  • Stop talking to the person you met online if they are starts asking you take a picture of yourself.
  • Don't let them manipulate you.
  • If they bring up a topic about adult stuff, block them immediately.

How would you identify the Cyber Groomer?

  • Someone send you lots of messages.
  • Ask you to keep your conversations secret.
  • Try to find out more.
  • Start sending you sexual messages.
  • Get you to share personal information.
  • Try to blackmail you.
